Hello! I'm Amanda...

but some folks call me a Rhinicorn. Why? Great question.

Enter the Unicorn.

I have worked with tech in some capacity for over 20 years. I love the devices and functionality as much as I love interactions with the people who use them. This mix of personality and tech-ability has been referenced as a unicorn - mythical and impossible to find.


Enter the Rhino.

Fostering my Clifton Futuristic theme, I practice the Rhino Principle (Paul Johnson). I envision future possibilities, pick one, determine how to get there, and go. The Rhino - practical, determined, and a force of nature.


Bridging this breadth is my specialty. One foot in each realm. A Rhinicorn.

I'm driven by learners.

Specifically - online learner. This modern breed of learner seeks to advance future-ready skills, problem-solving mindsets, and meaningful opportunities for career advancement. To achieve this goal, a multi-faceted approach to transforming how they "experience learning" is required. Online learning has made its way into the forefront of our training and education landscape. We should embrace it and utilize its flexibility to broaden the reach of education to all potential learners.
